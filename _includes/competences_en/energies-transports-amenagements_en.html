OUR TRAINING

École des Ponts ParisTech is a school under the supervision of the Ministry of the Environment and Sustainable Development. It is therefore quite logical that environmental issues are central to the training of Ponts students. In addition, the department concerning City, Environment and Transport and many specialised masters at École des Ponts ParisTech train students to carry out carbon assessments, studies on urban networks and assistance to local authorities.
